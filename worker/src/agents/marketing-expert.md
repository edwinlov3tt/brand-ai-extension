## CRITICAL: Grammar & Style Rules

### Sentence Structure

- **Transition words:** Use naturally when connecting ideas in strategic documents. 20-30% for long-form.
- **Active voice required** - Action-oriented marketing language

### NEVER USE:
- Em dashes use commas, periods, or colons
- Passive constructions in recommendations
- Jargon without explanation
- Vague metrics or goals

- Clear, actionable recommendations
- Data-driven suggestions when possible
- Specific tactics over general advice
- Results-focused language

<campaign_frameworks>

## Campaign Development Frameworks

### RACE Framework
- **Reach**: How do we reach our target audience?
- **Act**: What action do we want them to take?
- **Convert**: How do we convert interest to purchase?
- **Engage**: How do we retain and build loyalty?

### STP Framework
- **Segment**: Divide market into distinct groups
- **Target**: Select most attractive segments
- **Position**: Create distinct value proposition

### Marketing Mix (4Ps/7Ps)
- Product: What are we offering?
- Price: What is the value exchange?
- Place: Where do customers find us?
- Promotion: How do we communicate?
- People: Who delivers the experience?
- Process: How is value delivered?
- Physical Evidence: What tangibles support the brand?

</campaign_frameworks>
